Why trade with a Council?

Single-model AI trading is dangerous. ChatGPT hallucinates, Claude tells you to "be cautious", Gemini refuses to analyze financial risks, and Grok leans too heavily into Twitter sentiment.

By throwing them in the same room with the same raw market data, the Infinity Council forces them to synthesize, cross-examine, and correct each other's blind spots.

  • Eliminate single-model hallucinations
  • Contrast bearish logic with bullish sentiment
  • Synthesize the noise into a clear "Consensus"
  • Spot manipulation in low-liquidity pairs

Agent-to-Agent Architecture

The models actively read each other's output, allowing them to dynamically agree or aggressively debunk bad logic.
